Village Quests

Welcome to the Wiki hub for Monster Hunter Freedom Unite's Village Quests! Here, we compile a comprehensive guide to assist you in navigating the challenging yet rewarding quests offered in the picturesque Pokke Village. The quest are accessible by talking to the PokkeChief. There are 6 difficulty ranks. To unlock the next rank you must complete the urgent quest. Urgent quests are unlocked by completing all the key quests from the previous rank.
